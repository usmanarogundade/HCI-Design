Frequently Asked Questions

How cold does Winnipeg get during winter?

According to Wikipedia.com, Winnipeg's winter is the tenth coldest out of Canada's 100 largest cities. With an average low of −20.2 °C (−4.4 °F). From December through February the maximum daily temperature exceeds 0 °C (32 °F), on average, for only 10 days and the minimum daily temperature falls below −20 °C (−4 °F) on 49 days.

What is the minimum wage in Winnipeg?

The current minimum wage in Winnipeg is $10.70, coming up from $10.40. Hence making Manitoba the fourth highest paying province among other Canadian jurisdictions

Can I use my foreign driver's license?

If you plan on staying in Winnipeg for less than 6 months, you can use your international license, else you will need to get a new provincial drivers license from the Manitoba Public Insurance body.

What is the most vibrant part of the city?

The Osborne and Exchange district happen to be the most vibrant part of the city, as they boast of the best restaurants, events, and a ever growing population of fun lovers.

Where can I see the Winnipeg Jets play?

You can either get tickets to see a Jets game online or by going to the MTS Centre that is located downtown Winnipeg.
